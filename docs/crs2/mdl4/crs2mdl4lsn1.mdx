Whenever you see a shocking or unbelievable photo, stop before sharing. Use reverse image search to see if it’s old, edited, or out of context. Often, you’ll find the image has been circulating for years under different claims.
- Verify shocking or viral images
- Check if an image is old, edited, or taken out of context
- Compare with credible news sources
